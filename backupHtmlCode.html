About Sooty

As you are probably already aware, Sooty is a black miniature Schnauzer with a big persona and even bigger heart!

Here are a few things you should know about Sooty, he loves:

  • Chewing on plastic bottles
  • To roll around in fox poo
  • Long walks
  • Chasing birds

Sooty's Charachteristics

Sooty is a loving and clever companion with a glossy coat and a touch of independence. His playful and crazy side adds a delightful charm to his loyal and intelligent personality, making him a joy for anyone lucky enough to have him by their side.

Sooty's Favourite Food

Like most dogs, Sooty likes almost everything! But here are some of his favourite foods!

  • Chicken
  • Watermelon
  • Tripe (smells horrible)

Things Sooty does not like:

  1. Fireworks
  2. When his best friends (us) leave him :(
  3. Vets
